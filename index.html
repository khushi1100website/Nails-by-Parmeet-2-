How Nails by Parmeet Came into Existence

Founded during the lockdown, when the world slowed down and everything was uncertain, Parmeet discovered her passion for beauty and self-expression. In the quiet of isolation, she immersed herself in the world of nail artistry — researching, learning, perfecting.

With a professional certification and unstoppable passion, she transformed her dream into reality, creating stunning nail art for every occasion.

Nails by Parmeet is a story of hope, creativity, and turning passion into purpose.
